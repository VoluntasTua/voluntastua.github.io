The Religion of the Month Club Podcast is Coming

The first two episodes of the Religion of the Month Club have been recorded and are scheduled to come out on רֹאשׁ חודש שְׁבָט (Rosh Chodesh Sh’vat 5781, or January 13) and 農曆新年 (Chinese Lunar New Year, or February 12) respectively.

Episodes will come out on the first of every lunar month as designated by the tradition associated with the text. For the first six months, we will focus on the first chapter of the each text (with some exceptions), e.g. the first chapter of Genesis (בְּרֵאשִׁית‎), the first chapter of the Tao De Ching (道德經), the first chapter of the Qur’an (القرآن‎), etc.
